DANCE

It is an art form that is performed by dancers, as a solo or a group dance. Dancing is frequently accompanied with music and can be used for expression of thoughts or emotion, has a cultural or religious value, for entertainment and also for the health for healing and as exercise.

HISTORY

Archaeology delivers traces of dance from prehistoric times such as the 30,000-year-old Bhimbetka rock shelters paintings in India and Egyptian tomb paintings depicting dancing figures from c. 3300BC. Many contemporary dance forms can be traced back to historical, traditional, ceremonial, and ethnic dances of the ancient period.
